MINDFULNESS MEDITATION

For the past 20 years, meditating has been a central part of my life. I have maintained a regular mindfulness meditation practice and attended many meditation retreats, including several fully silent retreats of one-month of longer. As well, I have taken in-depth courses on meditation practice and philosophy and recieved mindfulness meditation teacher training. For the past several years, I have taught mindfulness meditation courses and led meditation groups on a by-donation basis.


Cultivating a regular mindfulness practice has helped me to overcome personal challenges, develop strategies for managing stress and anxiety, and become more empathetic and emotionally mature. When I interact with others, I am better able to stay present and authentic and to communicate directly and compassionately. In work, school, and volunteer settings, my practice has enabled me to navigate conflicts and high-pressure situations with greater ease and groundedness.


I am always happy to discuss meditation and its benefits, teach and lead meditaion groups, or provide individual instruction and feedback to anyone who is interested.
